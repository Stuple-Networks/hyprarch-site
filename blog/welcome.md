# Welcome to the HyprArch Project!

This is the first official blog post for **HyprArch**. We are building a streamlined, aesthetic Arch Linux experience centered around the Hyprland tiling window manager.

### What's coming next?
* **Beta ISOs:** We're working on the first public build.
* **Documentation:** Detailed guides on custom keybinds and waybar config.
* **Community:** Setting up our Discord/Matrix bridge.

Stay tuned for more updates!
